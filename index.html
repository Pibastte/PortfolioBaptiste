Quick presentation

I am a french engineering student at ESILV Paris where I study computer science. I am also a startup founder at Freckly where I use my programming skills to develop the python back-end of a web application, I am also in charge of training a machine learning model (CNN) for image analysis. I am always seeking for new opportunities to further develop my skills.

Motivations

It has been now 4 years since I started programming, overcomming important challenges is what drives me to continue building things. My goal is to one day make a real impact in society with the skills I acquire, it's in this sense that I started Freckly with 2 friends. I am now also looking for an internship to further improve my hard and soft skills in a professional environment. I am open to opportunities all over the world.

Personal story

"Last summer in the middle of a web project I had to change the deployment plateform I was working on to AWS. I had no experience in deploying web applications to the cloud. To tackle this challenge I moved back to my appartment in Paris 10 days before school started, mainly to have access to a good bandwidth, I was excited about this challenge. I thought it would take me a couple days to tackle this problem, it turned out I had to dive pretty deep into the AWS' documentation and had to try countless configuration in order to figure out what worked and what did not. In the end it took me 8 days to achieve the goal I had fixed. I continualy refused the temptation to quit and I was rewarded for it. The joy that came out of it was thrilling, the webapp I was building was more responsive than ever and I had gained knowledge in a new domain. I am actively looging to live similar experiences in the future."
